Quota Share Reinsurance

The reinsurer accepts a fixed fraction q of every covered risk in the treaty portfolio.

Z = q · X  (ceded) | Y = (1 − q) · X  (retained)
q ∈ (0,1) is the quota share cession rate, X is the claim amount.

Surplus Reinsurance

Cession is risk-dependent. Only the amount above the cedant's retention M is ceded, up to treaty capacity.

q(S) = max(0, 1 − M / S)
Z = q(S) · X  (ceded) | Y = X − Z  (retained)
S = sum insured, M = cedant retention, X = claim amount.
